Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Welcome to the Cloud community!

Cloud is a location-based social networking app where people discover places, join conversations, and connect through shared interests. Our community thrives when everyone feels safe, respected, and welcome.

These Community Guidelines explain what we expect from all users. By using Cloud, you agree to follow these guidelines and our Terms of Service.

Our Values

Cloud is built on these core principles:

  • Respect: Treat others as you'd like to be treated
  • Safety: Create a welcoming environment free from harassment
  • Authenticity: Be genuine and honest in your interactions
  • Community: Help build positive connections around shared places

Reporting Violations

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines:

  1. Report it: Use the in-app report feature on messages or user profiles
  2. Block if needed: You can block users to prevent them from contacting you
  3. Provide details: Help us understand the violation when reporting

All reports are reviewed by our team. False or abusive reports may result in action against your account.

Enforcement

When we find violations of these guidelines, we may take action including:

  • Warning: First-time or minor violations
  • Content Removal: Deleting violating messages or profiles
  • Temporary Suspension: Restricting account access for a period
  • Permanent Ban: Permanently terminating the account
  • Legal Action: Reporting illegal activity to authorities
Severity matters: Serious violations (illegal content, child safety issues, threats of violence) result in immediate account termination and may be reported to law enforcement.

Appeals

If you believe your content or account was actioned by mistake:

  1. Email us at hi.clouderapp@gmail.com
  2. Include your username and details about the action
  3. Explain why you believe it was a mistake
  4. We'll review within 7 business days

Group Chat Guidelines

Group chats based on places have additional expectations:

  • Stay on topic: Keep discussions relevant to the place
  • Be respectful: Different people have different opinions
  • No spam: Don't flood chats with promotional content
  • Welcome newcomers: Help new members feel included
